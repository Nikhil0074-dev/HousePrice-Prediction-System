How to Read These Metrics
MAE – Mean Absolute Error
Average prediction error in lakhs. Lower = better. A MAE of 7 means predictions are off by ~₹7L on average.
RMSE – Root Mean Squared Error
Penalises large errors more. Lower = better. More sensitive to outliers than MAE.
R² Score
How well the model explains price variance. 1.0 = perfect. 0.82 means 82% of price variation is explained.
